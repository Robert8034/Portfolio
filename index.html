About

Who am I?

Hi, my name is Robert Dams and I am a Software Engineer with a passion for both technology and people. As a student I always liked exploring new things and learning whenever I could, be it programming languages (C#, Java, C++) or development methods (Test Driven Development, Scrum). Studying and eventually graduating at Fontys Hogescholen gave me the opportunity to do so. Over the years this has resulted in a can-do attitude and a passion to learn new things. I like a challenge and approach problems with a positive mindset. Coming from an environment full of engineers and inventors, I take great satisfaction and pride when I bring a vision or idea to life through software!

Skills

Software Development is my bread and butter. I am proficient in both high level programming languages like C# and Java, as well as lower level ones like C++. All code adhers to the latest SOLID principles.

Development Operations

DevOps is second nature to me. I have experience in both Azure and Google Cloud as well as code analysis tools like Jenkins and SonarQube.

Project management
