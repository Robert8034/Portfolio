About

Who am I?

My name is Robert Dams, and I am currently a Software Engineering student at Fontys Hogescholen. I am a curious, communicative and motivated person in the software development field.

As a person I am open-minded and I like to learn. This results in me exploring lots of different aspects of Software Engineering instead of sticking to one subject. I try out new programming languages and techniques in my free time to expand my view on Software Engineering.

I am a people's person and greatly enjoy working with or for others. As a bit of a social butterfly you will find me mostly taking on leadership roles in a group and handling communication to stakeholders.

Skills

Software Development is my bread and butter. I am proficient in both high level programming languages like C# and Java, as well as lower level ones like C++. All code adhers to the latest SOLID principles.

Project management
