About

Who am I?

Hi, I’m Robert Dams — a Software Engineer and Scrum Master based in the Brainport Eindhoven region. I’m passionate about technology and how people interact with it, always driven by curiosity and a love for learning. Over the years, I’ve worked with a range of technologies including C#, Blazor, React, Azure, GitHub Enterprise (GHE), Java, and C++. I enjoy applying Agile and Scrum principles in my work, along with Test Driven Development, to build reliable, scalable, and user-focused software solutions.

I’m driven by a can-do attitude and a love for tackling new challenges. I approach every project with a positive mindset and the determination to see things through. Coming from a family of engineers and inventors, I’ve always enjoyed turning ideas into real, working solutions through software.

In my career, I’ve faced all kinds of challenges that helped shape who I am as a developer. My early years as a consultant taught me how to quickly adapt to different company cultures, communicate clearly with clients, and manage expectations through well-defined plans and regular updates. Often working independently, I also took on leadership roles — guiding teams and helping other developers navigate transitions within their projects.

Over the past year, I’ve strengthened these skills by earning my Professional Scrum Master I (PSM I) certification from Scrum.org and taking on a hybrid role as both Software Engineer and Scrum Master for my team. This experience has deepened my understanding of Agile practices and scalable software development.

Skills

Software Development

Software development is not just my profession; it's my passion and expertise. I excel in high-level programming languages such as C# and Java, while also delving into the intricacies of lower-level languages like C++. In every line of code I craft, I strictly adhere to the latest SOLID principles, ensuring robust and maintainable software solutions.

Development Operations

DevOps is ingrained in my work approach. I possess extensive experience in Azure and Github Enterprise, complemented by proficiency in code analysis tools like Jenkins and SonarQube.

Webdesign

A robust back-end should always be complemented by an elegant front-end. My expertise extends to React and Blazor, and I excel in harnessing the power of Bootstrap, whether in React or native HTML/CSS, to craft visually appealing and highly functional user interfaces.

Cyber Security

Security is at the core of my software design and development philosophy. Every application I create is fortified against a wide spectrum of cyber threats, including but not limited to SQL Injection and Cross-Site Scripting, ensuring the safety and integrity of the systems I build.

Project management

I’ve taken on the role of project leader multiple times and, in recent years, have served as Scrum Master for my team — consistently delivering projects on time and to a high standard. Working with me means clear, efficient communication and a proactive, solution-oriented mindset focused on achieving results.

SQA

Each code release undergoes meticulous testing, encompassing multiple layers of evaluation, whether through DevOps pipelines or rigorous manual testing during development. Moreover, my extensive experience in Test Driven Development reinforces the quality and reliability of the codebase.
