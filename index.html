About

Who am I?

Greetings, I'm Robert Dams, a Software Engineer driven by a deep appreciation for technology and the dynamic interplay of human interaction with it. Throughout my academic journey, I thrived on exploration and continuous learning. Whether it was mastering programming languages such as C#, Java, and C++, or embracing development methodologies like Test Driven Development and Scrum, my educational path at Fontys Hogescholen provided the perfect backdrop for this intellectual adventure.

This relentless pursuit of knowledge has bestowed upon me a resolute can-do attitude and a fervor for embracing fresh challenges. I bring to the table a mindset rooted in positivity and an unwavering determination to surmount obstacles. Growing up in an environment teeming with engineers and inventors, I've come to relish the sense of fulfillment and pride that accompanies transforming a vision or concept into tangible reality through the art of software development.

Throughout my career, I've confronted a wide array of challenges. In my formative years as a consultant, I honed my ability to swiftly adapt to varying company cultures. This entailed not only adapting but also mastering the art of effective communication with clients, ensuring precise roadmaps and keeping them well-informed about my progress. In this consultant role, I often navigated these challenges independently, necessitating proficient leadership and guidance of fellow software developers during transitional phases within each organization.

Skills

Software Development

Software development is not just my profession; it's my passion and expertise. I excel in high-level programming languages such as C# and Java, while also delving into the intricacies of lower-level languages like C++. In every line of code I craft, I strictly adhere to the latest SOLID principles, ensuring robust and maintainable software solutions.

Development Operations

DevOps is ingrained in my work approach. I possess extensive experience in Azure and Google Cloud, complemented by proficiency in code analysis tools like Jenkins and SonarQube.

Webdesign

A robust back-end should always be complemented by an elegant front-end. My expertise extends to React and Blazor, and I excel in harnessing the power of Bootstrap, whether in React or native HTML/CSS, to craft visually appealing and highly functional user interfaces.

Cyber Security

Security is at the core of my software design and development philosophy. Every application I create is fortified against a wide spectrum of cyber threats, including but not limited to SQL Injection and Cross-Site Scripting, ensuring the safety and integrity of the systems I build.

Project management

I've assumed the role of project leader on numerous occasions, consistently delivering projects on time. Collaborating with me guarantees efficient and timely communication, along with a proactive, solution-oriented approach to challenges.

SQA

Each code release undergoes meticulous testing, encompassing multiple layers of evaluation, whether through DevOps pipelines or rigorous manual testing during development. Moreover, my extensive experience in Test Driven Development reinforces the quality and reliability of the codebase.
